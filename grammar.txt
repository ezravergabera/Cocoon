expr is the root. The root has the least priority.
expr    : DTYPE:(num | number) ID ASSIGN expr
        : term ((PLUS | MINUS) term)*

term    : factor ((MUL | DIV | INTDIV | MOD) factor)*

factor  : (PLUS|MINUS) factor
        : power

power   : atom (EXPO factor)*

atom    : INT | FLOAT | ID
        : LPAREN expr RPAREN
